Erin Ijesha, Osun State
Erin-Ijesha Waterfalls is located in Erin-Ijesha. It is a tourist attraction located in Oriade local government area, Osun State, Nigeria.
Tarkwa Bay, Lagos State
Tarkwa Bay is an artificial sheltered beach located near the Lagos state harbour in Nigeria. Due to its island status, it is only accessible by boat or water taxis.

Yankari National Park is a large wildlife park located in the south-central part of Bauchi State, in north eastern Nigeria.
